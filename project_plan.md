# Lakshmi Oral & Dental Health Care Website

## 1. Project Description
A professional multi-page dental clinic website for "Lakshmi Oral & Dental Health Care" located in Bangalore, India. The website showcases dental services, the clinic's story, team, and allows patients to book appointments. Target audience: local patients in Bangalore seeking quality dental care. Core value: Build trust through professional presentation and easy appointment booking.

## 2. Page Structure
- `/` — Home (Hero, Services Overview, Why Choose Us, Stats, Testimonials, CTA)
- `/about` — About Us (Story, Mission/Vision, Team, Values)
- `/services` — Services (Detailed service list, Treatment Process, FAQ)
